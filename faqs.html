Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register on IBT SUB?

To register, simply click on the "Sign Up" or "Register" button on our homepage. You'll need to provide some basic information like your name, email, and phone number, and create a password.

Is registration free?

Yes, registration on IBT SUB is absolutely free. You only pay for the services you use.

How do I log in to my account?

Visit our homepage and click on "Login." Enter your registered email/username and password to access your dashboard.

What if I forget my password?

On the login page, click on "Forgot Password?" and follow the instructions to reset it via your registered email address.

How can I fund my wallet?

You can fund your wallet using various secure payment methods, including bank transfer, debit/credit cards, and USSD. Navigate to the "Fund Wallet" section on your dashboard for options.

What is the minimum amount I can fund my wallet with?

The minimum funding amount may vary, but it is typically small (e.g., ₦100 or ₦500). Please check the "Fund Wallet" page for the exact current minimum.

How long does it take for my wallet to be funded after payment?

Payments are usually processed instantly. If there's a delay, please allow up to 15 minutes, then contact our support with your transaction details.

Are there any charges for funding my wallet?

Some payment gateways may apply a small processing fee. This will be clearly displayed before you confirm your payment.

How do I buy data bundles?

Go to the "Buy Data" section, select your network, choose your desired data plan, enter the recipient's phone number, and confirm your purchase.

How do I buy airtime?

Navigate to the "Buy Airtime" section, select the network, enter the amount and phone number, then complete the transaction.

Can I pay electricity bills through your platform?

Yes, you can pay electricity bills. Go to the "Electricity Bills" section, select your Disco, meter type, enter your meter number, amount, and phone number.

How do I subscribe for cable TV (DSTV, GOtv, Startimes)?

In the "Cable Subscription" section, choose your cable provider, enter your Smartcard/IUC number, select your desired plan, and proceed with payment.

What are Education Pins and how do I purchase them?

Education Pins are scratch cards or tokens for examination bodies like WAEC, NECO, and NABTEB. Select the exam type in the "Education Pins" section, the price will automatically appear, and then confirm your purchase.

How does the Airtime to Cash service work?

This service allows you to convert excess airtime on your phone into cash in your bank account. You transfer the airtime to a designated number, and we credit the equivalent cash amount (minus our commission) to your wallet or bank account.

What is the conversion rate for Airtime to Cash?

The conversion rate varies by network and amount, typically ranging from 80% to 90% as shown in our pricing plans. This means if you convert ₦1000 airtime at an 80% rate, you receive ₦800 cash.

What are the different user tiers (Smart User, Affiliate, Special Package)?

These are different account levels that offer varying discounts and benefits on our services. Higher tiers generally receive better discounts and conversion rates.

How can I upgrade my account tier?

Information on upgrading your account tier and the requirements for each will be available in the "Pricing Plans" or "Upgrade Account" section of your dashboard.

My transaction failed, but my wallet was debited. What should I do?

In most cases, failed transactions are automatically reversed within minutes. If not, please contact our support team immediately with your transaction ID and details.

How can I contact customer support?

You can reach our customer support team via the "Contact Us" page, email, or live chat (if available) during business hours.

Is my personal and payment information secure on IBT SUB?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal data and payment information. We do not store your full card details.
